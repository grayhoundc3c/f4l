Truth About Hip-Hop

Rob Ibarra(Born on June 18, 1984 better known by his stage name Westcoast Truth Westcoast Truth Producer/Artist planted his feet in San Bernardino, California and is continually spreading The Truth About Hip-Hop throughout the Music Industry.

At the age of 14 Westcoast Truth was devoted to be a Producer/Artist, Since then Westcoast Truth has established a hometown Music Studio BlueMoon Recordz which has released several mix tapes such as The Truth About Hip-Hop Volume 1, The Truth About Hip-Hop Volume 2, Ima tell you how it is Volume 1, and Single Mastered Tracks like I Got it Ft. Locsta,Welcome to Dino, Copy Catz and Westcoast Truth latest How We Do Things Ft. Select One. Music Video's are filmed through the 909 Dino.

Known to his fans as Westcoast Truth Producer/Artist, this remarkable 909 Dino Rapper is also a devoted family man, contributor to the community, and a inspiration to todays youth. What sets Westcoast Truth a step above the rest is his Athletic ability, knowledge of sports and takes the time to Coach, Play and Volunteer teams all year around.

Special thanks to everyone that shows their love & support...909 STAND UP
